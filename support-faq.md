## How ImmaShap Works

1. Sign in with your Google account.
2. Enter your name in Settings.
3. Add one or more emergency contacts by entering their name and email address. They will receive a notification to accept your request.
4. Set your check-in frequency — how often you need to tap the ImmaShap button to confirm you are okay.
5. If you miss your check-in, ImmaShap sends you reminder notifications. If you miss all reminders, your emergency contacts are automatically alerted.
6. You can also send an immediate emergency alert by long-pressing the ImmaShap button, or let your contacts know you are safe by tapping "I Am Safe Now".

### Getting Started

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**

A: No separate account is needed. ImmaShap uses your existing Google account to sign in. Simply tap "Sign in with Google" and select your Google account.

**Q: My emergency contact is not receiving my connection request. What do I do?**

A: Check the following:
- You must enter the **Gmail address your contact uses to sign into ImmaShap** — not just any email address they own. ImmaShap uses Google Sign-In, so only their Google/Gmail address will work.
- Make sure you entered their email address correctly. Email addresses are stored in lowercase — if there is a mismatch in capitalisation it will not match.
- Make sure your contact has ImmaShap installed and is signed in with that same Google account.
- Ask them to check their notification settings and make sure ImmaShap notifications are enabled on their device.

**Q: Can I add more than one emergency contact?**

A: Yes. You can add multiple emergency contacts. All confirmed contacts will receive an alert if you miss your check-ins.

### Notifications

**Q: I am not receiving notifications. What should I check?**

A: Work through the following steps:

1. **Check notification permissions** — Go to your phone's Settings → Apps →  → Notifications → make sure all notifications are turned on.
2. **Check battery optimisation** — This is the most common cause of missed notifications. See the section below on Battery Optimisation.
3. **Check your internet connection** — Notifications require an active internet connection to be received.
4. **Reinstall the app** — If nothing else works, uninstall and reinstall . This refreshes your device's notification token.

**Q: My contacts are not receiving emergency alerts.**

A: Check the following:
- Confirm the connection request was accepted and shows as "Confirmed" in your contacts list.
- Ask your contact to check their notification settings for .
- Ask your contact to check their battery optimisation settings (see below).
- Make sure your contact's device has an active internet connection.

**Q: I accepted a contact request but still show as "Pending".**

A: Close and reopen the app. The status updates in real time but may occasionally need a refresh.

### Check-ins and Alerts

**Q: I accidentally triggered an emergency alert. What do I do?**

A: Tap the "I Am Safe Now" button immediately. This sends a follow-up notification to all your contacts letting them know you are safe and dismisses the alert banner on their devices.

**Q: The app is not checking in automatically — I have to open it every time.**

A:  does not check in automatically — you must tap the  button yourself to record a check-in. This is intentional. The app monitors whether you have checked in, not whether you are physically active.

**Q: How do I change how often I need to check in?**

A: Tap the gear icon in the bottom right corner to open Settings. Adjust the "Check-in Frequency" to your preferred interval.

**Q: How exactly do reminders and emergency alerts work?**

A: When you set a check-in frequency, ImmaShap calculates your deadline — the time by which you must tap the ImmaShap button. Before that deadline, reminder notifications fire at regular intervals to prompt you to check in.

For example, with the default settings (3 reminders, 1 hour interval, 1 day frequency):
- Reminder 1 fires 3 hours before your deadline
- Reminder 2 fires 2 hours before your deadline
- Reminder 3 fires 1 hour before your deadline
- If you still have not checked in after your deadline passes **and** all 3 reminders have fired, your emergency contacts are automatically alerted

**Important:** The emergency alert only fires once all reminders have been sent and the deadline has passed. Missing the deadline alone is not enough — all reminders must have fired first. This means the earliest an alert can fire is after the deadline plus enough time for all reminders to have been scheduled.

You can adjust the number of reminders and the interval between them in Settings.

**Q: What happens if I go somewhere without internet?**

A:  requires an internet connection to send and receive alerts. If you are going somewhere without connectivity, check in before you lose signal and inform your contacts manually.  is not designed for use in areas without internet access.

### Contacts

**Q: How do I remove an emergency contact?**

A: Open Settings → find the contact under "Your Contacts" → tap the red minus button. Your contact will receive a notification that they have been removed.

**Q: Someone added me as their emergency contact. How do I remove myself?**

A: Open Settings → find the person under "Your Watchlist" → tap the red minus button.

**Q: I declined a contact request by mistake. What do I do?**

A: Ask the person to send you a new contact request. Declined requests cannot be reinstated — a new request must be created.

### Privacy and Data

**Q: What data does  store?**

A:  stores your name, email address, and a device notification token in our secure Firebase database. Check-in settings are stored locally on your device. See our full [Privacy Policy] for details.

**Q: Can I delete my account and data?**

A: Yes. Open the app → tap the gear icon → scroll down → tap **Delete Account**. This permanently deletes your account, all your data, and all your connections from our servers. This action cannot be undone. If you are unable to access the app, contact us at ImmaShap@AxiomArray.co.za and we will remove your data within 30 days.

**Q: Does ImmaShap track my location?**

A: No. ImmaShap does not access, store, or share your location at any time.
